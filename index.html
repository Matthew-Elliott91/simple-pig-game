How to Play Pig (Player vs. Computer)

The game of Pig is a simple dice game played between you and the computer. Here's how it works:

  • Players take turns rolling a dice as many times as they wish, adding the dice's value to their score for the turn.
  • If you roll a 1, your turn ends, and you lose all the points you accumulated in that turn.
  • You can choose to "Hold" to add the points from your turn to your total score and pass the dice to the computer.
  • The first player to reach 100 points wins the game.
  • The computer plays automatically, making its decisions based on a simple strategy.
